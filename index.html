Man Utd Young Stars
Kobbie Boateng Mainoo (born 19 April 2005) is an English professional footballer who plays as a midfielder[3] for Premier League club Manchester United. Mainoo is a graduate of Manchester United's youth system and won the Jimmy Murphy Young Player of the Year award in 2023. He made his first-team debut in an EFL Cup game in January 2023. Mainoo has played youth international football for England at under-17, under-18 and under-19 levels.Early life Born in Stockport, Greater Manchester,[4] Mainoo started his youth career at Cheadle & Gatley Junior Football Club, before joining Manchester United at the age of nine.[5] He is the younger brother of Jordan Hames, who appeared as a contestant on the fifth series of Love Island.[6]
